Delete Account Request

Request deletion of your Makeup Seven Artist account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to official.makeupseven@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete Account Request.
  3. Include your registered mobile number and registered email address.
  4. Our support team will review and process your request.

Support Email

official.makeupseven@gmail.com

Email Deletion Request

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your artist account profile information
  • Your listed services and availability details
  • Your portfolio content associated with the account, subject to operational and legal limits

What May Be Retained

  • Transaction, tax, or compliance-related records where retention is required by law
  • Limited internal records needed to resolve disputes, prevent fraud, or enforce legal obligations

Processing Time

We aim to review deletion requests within a reasonable period after identity verification and required compliance checks.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about data deletion, privacy, or account support, contact us at official.makeupseven@gmail.com.
